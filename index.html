iColor - Make Color Effect Magic & Change Color

iColor let you can change color of anything in image to create a dramatic look.
You can use iColor to create black/white or any color image but keep colored area to get people's attention on the area you want.
With app, you can use to:
-change skin color
- change hair color
- or change background color in image and more which belong to user's creativity.
By using brush tool/eraser and zoom/move tool and color effect to work on detail pixel-accuracy.

Choose photo from Library or Camera -> iColor -> Share edited image to your friend via Facebook,Twitter, Email or Save to Album.
